About Me:

My name is Oluwakemisola Sophia Bakare. My journey into Frontend Engineering began with a passion for nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning.
With a background in Early Years Montessori education, I have spent years honing my skills as a guide, creating environments that promote independence, curiosity, and self-discovery.
Throughout my career, I have witnessed the transformative impact of personalized learning on children's development, instilling in me a deep-seated belief in the power of education to unlock human potential.

However, as I navigated the landscape of traditional education, I became increasingly aware of the disparities that exist in access to quality learning experiences.
Inequities in resource allocation, geographic limitations, and socioeconomic barriers all served to hinder the realization of every child's right to education.
It was this realization that sparked my journey into front-end engineering, as I sought to leverage technology as a tool for educational innovation and social change.
By combining my educational expertise with technical skills, I am dedicated to creating digital solutions that bridge gaps in education and provide equitable learning opportunities for all.
